It's me, Kiah

I'm a Front-end Developer with a passion for all things design, development and empowerment.

As a recent graduate of General Assembly’s Web Development Immersive course my immersion into web development and the tech space has been truly life changing. With no direct connection to the industry prior to starting the course, I was at a dead end when it came to my career trajectory. I went out on a limb to pursue an industry and career that is dynamic and inclusionary.


Combining my skills in Front-end Development whilst leveraging my graphic design experience, I enjoy creating visually appealing products with a strong focus on user experience. I believe in growth, product market fit and have an early adoption mentality.


I am a big believer in the tech industry as a key motivator to underprivileged and low socio-economic education and I am currently exploring an opportunity with the education department to establish charitable talks and contributions to bring awareness and motivation to those who would otherwise not have exposure to this type of learning.


I am passionate about the impact of IoT and LED wearables in everyday life and enjoy learning more about the technology involved. I have created an LED wearables dress to understand the technology used and this has allowed me to explore creative ideas regarding the combination of fashion and technology.
